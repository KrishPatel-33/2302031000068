Pick Your Science Adventure!

Volcano

  1. Place the cola bottle on a flat surface.
  2. Open the bottle carefully.
  3. Drop 3-5 Mentos into the cola.
  4. Step back and watch the eruption!

Oobleck

  1. Mix 1 cup of water with 2 cups of cornstarch.
  2. Stir until it forms a thick paste.
  3. Press hard—it feels solid!
  4. Let go—it flows like liquid!

Self-Inflating Balloon

  1. Pour 2-3 tablespoons of vinegar into a bottle.
  2. Put 1-2 teaspoons of baking soda into a balloon.
  3. Stretch the balloon over the bottle’s mouth.
  4. Lift the balloon, letting the baking soda fall in—watch it inflate!

Lemon Battery

  1. Insert one zinc nail and one copper nail into a lemon.
  2. Connect a wire to each nail.
  3. Attach the wires to a small LED or buzzer.
  4. Watch as the lemon generates electricity!

Safety Guidelines

Wear safety goggles, hairnet, lab coat and gloves.

Strictly no food and drinks.

Do lab experiments with permission or supervision.

Wash your hands before and after using the laboratory.

Be careful with glassware and flammable objects.

Maintain the cleanliness of your workspace.
